Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I upload a PDF or XML file?

To upload a file, simply click the "Upload" button on the homepage and follow the prompts.

How do I generate a mind map from my document?

After uploading your document, DocuMap will automatically process it and generate an interactive mind map.
